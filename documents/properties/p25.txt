# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-025
## Two-Bedroom Apartment Near Central Railway Station in Center, Sofia

### Overview

This two-bedroom apartment is located **within walking distance of Sofia Central Railway Station**, making it ideal for frequent travellers or professionals who commute by train. The property is on the third floor of a five-story building from 1970, with a refreshed facade (2019) and a practical layout. The area is central and well connected, with a mix of residential and commercial use.

The apartment is at **ul. Maria Luisa 45, Sofia Center, Sofia 1000**, a main street with excellent transport links. The asking price is **€91,000 (ninety-one thousand euros)**, offering good value for a central two-bedroom with transport access.
