# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-006
## Affordable One-Bedroom Apartment in Nadezhda, Sofia

---

### Overview

This practical one-bedroom apartment offers an affordable entry into the Sofia market in Nadezhda, a well-connected district with a strong sense of community and good local amenities. The property is on the first floor of a five-story panel building from 1982, which benefited from facade insulation and common-area upgrades in 2019. The layout is compact and efficient, suitable for a first-time buyer or an investor.

The apartment is located at **bl. 99, ul. Todor Kableshkov 15, Nadezhda, Sofia 1220**, a short walk from the district's main commercial and transport hub. The asking price is **€62,500 (sixty-two thousand five hundred euros)**, making it one of the most accessible options in the current portfolio for a legal, ready-to-use apartment.

The apartment is on the **1st floor out of 5**, with stairwell and elevator access. The building has an **intercom system** and **renewed entrance lobby**.
