# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-005
## Modern Two-Bedroom Apartment in Studentski Grad, Sofia

---

### Overview

This contemporary two-bedroom apartment is situated in Studentski Grad (Student City), a district known for its youthful atmosphere, university campuses, and good transport links to the rest of Sofia. The property is on the fifth floor of a seven-story building completed in 2010 and offers a functional layout and modern finishes, suitable for young professionals, small families, or investors seeking rental yield.

The apartment is located at **bl. 56, Studentski Grad, Sofia 1700**, within walking distance of Sofia University and the Technical University campuses. The asking price is **€95,000 (ninety-five thousand euros)**, offering strong value for a modern, well-maintained apartment in a high-demand rental area.

The total built-up area of the apartment is **72 square meters (sq. m.)**, with a usable living area of approximately **66 sq. m.** The layout comprises:

- **Two bedrooms**: Master bedroom (15 sq. m.) with south-facing window and built-in wardrobe; second bedroom (12 sq. m.) facing east, suitable as a bedroom, study, or home office.
- **Living room / dining area**: Open-plan space of 20 sq. m., with access to a 4 sq. m. balcony.
- **Kitchen**: Fitted kitchen, 8 sq. m., with standard cabinetry and space for appliances.
- **Bathroom**: Full bathroom with shower cabin (no bathtub), 5 sq. m., in good condition.

The apartment is on the **5th floor out of 7**, with **elevator access**. The building has an **intercom system** and **secure entrance**.

### Neighborhood & Location

Studentski Grad is a lively district centered on university life, with numerous cafés, bookshops, sports facilities, and affordable services. It is well connected by public transport and is popular with students, academics, and young professionals.

**Public transport** is a major advantage. The apartment is a **6-minute walk (approximately 450 meters)** from **Metro Station "Studentski Grad" (Line M2)** and close to bus and trolleybus lines serving the city center and other districts.

Nearby amenities within a 10-minute walk include:
- **Sofia University** and **Technical University** campuses
- **Studentski Grad commercial strip** — supermarkets (Kaufland, Billa), cafés, and shops
- **Sports facilities** and running routes in the district
- **Libraries and student services**
- **Pharmacies and medical practices**
- **Park "Zaimov"** and green areas
