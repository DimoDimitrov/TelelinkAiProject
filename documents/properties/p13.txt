# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-013
## One-Bedroom Apartment Near NDK and South Park in Lozenets, Sofia

---

### Overview

This practical one-bedroom apartment is ideally located **between the National Palace of Culture (NDK) and South Park**, offering easy access to events, green space, and the metro. The property is on the fifth floor of a six-story building from 1980, which was insulated and refurbished in 2019. The layout is efficient and the apartment has been well maintained by the current owner.

The apartment is at **ul. Atanas Dukov 12, Lozenets, Sofia 1164**, a quiet side street with quick access to the main boulevards. The asking price is **€84,500 (eighty-four thousand five hundred euros)**, an attractive price for a legal, move-in ready apartment in this sought-after area.
