# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-027
## Two-Bedroom Apartment with Panoramic View in Vitosha Area, Sofia

### Overview

This two-bedroom apartment offers a **panoramic view** over the city and toward Vitosha Mountain from its elevated position. The property is on the eighth floor of a ten-story building from 2007, in the Vitosha area (near Boyana/Dragalevtsi), with modern construction and excellent views. Ideal for those who appreciate natural light and vistas.

The apartment is at **ul. Vitosha 88, Vitosha Area, Sofia 1619**, a residential street with bus connection. The asking price is **€112,000 (one hundred twelve thousand euros)**, reflecting the views, building age, and location.
