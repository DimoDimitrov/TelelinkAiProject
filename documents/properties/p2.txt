# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-002
## Cozy One-Bedroom Apartment in Ivan Vazov, Sofia

---

### Overview

This charming one-bedroom apartment is situated in the heart of Ivan Vazov, one of Sofia's most central and vibrant districts. The property occupies the second floor of a well-preserved five-story building from the 1970s, recently modernized at the facade level, and offers a practical layout ideal for a single professional or a couple. Quiet building with predominantly owner-occupiers.

The apartment is located at **ul. Rakovski 118, Ivan Vazov, Sofia 1000**, within walking distance of Vitosha Boulevard and the main commercial and cultural hubs. The asking price is **€78,900 (seventy-eight thousand nine hundred euros)**, an attractive entry point for central Sofia given the location and recent interior refresh.

---

### Property Specifications

The total built-up area of the apartment is **52 square meters (sq. m.)**, with a usable living area of approximately **48 sq. m.** The layout comprises:

- **One bedroom**: A double bedroom of 14 sq. m., facing the quiet inner courtyard, with built-in wardrobe.
- **Living room**: Combined living and dining space of 18 sq. m., with a south-facing window and access to a 4 sq. m. balcony.
- **Kitchen**: Galley-style kitchen, 6 sq. m., with wall and base units and space for standard appliances.
- **Bathroom**: Full bathroom with bathtub, 5 sq. m., updated in 2022.
- **Entrance hallway**: 5 sq. m. with coat closet.

The apartment is on the **2nd floor out of 5**, with stairwell access only (no elevator). The building has an **intercom system** and a **renewed main entrance**.

### Neighborhood & Location

Ivan Vazov is a central district known for its mix of residential buildings, offices, cafés, and cultural institutions. The area is well served by public transport and on foot to the city center.

**Public transport** is excellent. The apartment is a **3-minute walk (approximately 250 meters)** from **Metro Station "Serdika" (Lines M1 and M2)**, with direct access to the entire metro network. Tram lines 5, 18, and 22, and multiple bus lines run on nearby boulevards.

Nearby amenities within a 5- to 10-minute walk include:
- **Vitosha Boulevard** — shopping, restaurants, and cafés
- **National Palace of Culture (NDK)** and South Park
- **Supermarkets** (Billa, Lidl, Kaufland)
- **Banks, pharmacies, and medical practices**
- **Theaters and galleries**
