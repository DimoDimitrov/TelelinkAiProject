# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-015
## Compact One-Bedroom Near Vitosha Boulevard in Center, Sofia

### Overview

This compact one-bedroom apartment is situated **within walking distance of Vitosha Boulevard** and the main commercial and cultural attractions of central Sofia. The property is on the fourth floor of a five-story building from 1968, with a refreshed facade (2018) and a practical layout suited to a single professional or a couple. The building is mixed residential and small offices, with a busy but central character.

The apartment is at **ul. Neofit Rilski 8, Sofia Center, Sofia 1000**, a side street off the main boulevards. The asking price is **€76,000 (seventy-six thousand euros)**, an entry-level price for a legal central Sofia apartment with good transport.

The apartment is on the **4th floor out of 5**. No elevator. **Intercom** and **refurbished entrance**.
