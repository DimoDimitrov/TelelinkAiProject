# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-003
## Family Three-Bedroom Apartment in Mladost 1, Sofia

---

### Overview

This spacious three-bedroom apartment is ideal for families seeking a quiet, well-connected location in one of Sofia's largest residential districts. The property is situated on the eighth floor of a ten-story panel building constructed in 1985, which underwent a full facade and common-area renovation in 2020. The layout offers clear separation between living and sleeping areas and benefits from plenty of natural light.

The apartment is located at **bl. 71A, ul. Andrey Lyapchev 1, Mladost 1, Sofia 1712**, within a short walk of green areas and the district's commercial strip. The asking price is **€142,000 (one hundred forty-two thousand euros)**, representing solid value for a large, renovated family apartment with parking in this area.

---

### Property Specifications

The total built-up area of the apartment is **112 square meters (sq. m.)**, with a usable living area of approximately **102 sq. m.** The layout comprises:

- **Three bedrooms**: Master bedroom (20 sq. m.) with south orientation and built-in wardrobe; second bedroom (15 sq. m.) facing east; third bedroom (12 sq. m.) suitable as a children's room or study.
- **Living room / dining area**: Large open-plan space of 28 sq. m., with access to a 6 sq. m. balcony overlooking the inner block.
- **Kitchen**: Fully equipped, 12 sq. m., with modern cabinetry and breakfast corner.
- **Bathroom**: Full bathroom with bathtub and shower over bath, 6 sq. m., renovated in 2020.
- **Separate WC**: 2.5 sq. m.
- **Entrance hallway**: 9 sq. m. with built-in storage.

The apartment is on the **8th floor out of 10**, with **elevator access**. The building has an **intercom system**, **video entry**, and **secure lobby**.

### Neighborhood & Location

Mladost 1 is a well-established residential district with a mix of panel and newer buildings, parks, schools, and commercial services. It is popular with families due to the abundance of kindergartens, schools, and playgrounds.

**Public transport** is convenient. The apartment is a **7-minute walk (approximately 550 meters)** from **Metro Station "Mladost 1" (Line M2)**, with direct links to the city center, Sofia Airport, and Business Park Sofia. Bus lines 94 and 120 serve the immediate vicinity.

Nearby amenities within a 10-minute walk include:
- **Mladost 1 commercial strip** — Kaufland, Billa, Lidl, pharmacies, and services
- **Park Mladost** and local playgrounds
- **Primary School 144** and several kindergartens
- **Medical center** and dental practices
- **Fitness clubs** and sports facilities
