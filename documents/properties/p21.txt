# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-021
## Two-Bedroom Period Apartment in Doctor's Garden (Vrazhdebna), Sofia

---

### Overview

This characterful two-bedroom apartment is in the **Doctor's Garden (Vrazhdebna)** area, a historic and green neighborhood near the city center with a mix of period buildings and villas. The property is on the second floor of a restored four-story building from 1938, with high ceilings, original moldings, and a recent quality renovation that preserves the period feel while ensuring modern comfort.

The apartment is at **ul. Ivan Geshov 14, Vrazhdebna, Sofia 1408**, a quiet, tree-lined street. The asking price is **€138,000 (one hundred thirty-eight thousand euros)**, reflecting the location, character, and renovation quality.

---

### Property Specifications

The total built-up area of the apartment is **88 square meters (sq. m.)**, with a usable living area of approximately **82 sq. m.** The layout comprises:

- **Two bedrooms**: Master (18 sq. m.) with high ceiling and built-in wardrobe, south-facing; second (14 sq. m.) facing the courtyard.
- **Living room / dining area**: 24 sq. m., with restored ceiling moldings and access to a 5 sq. m. balcony (street view).
- **Kitchen**: Modern fitted kitchen, 9 sq. m., with quality cabinetry and integrated appliances (2021).
- **Bathroom**: Full bathroom with bathtub and separate shower, 6 sq. m., fully renovated in 2021.
- **Entrance hallway**: 6 sq. m. with period tile detail and storage.

The apartment is on the **2nd floor out of 4**. No elevator. **Intercom** and **refurbished period entrance**.

### Neighborhood & Location

Vrazhdebna (Doctor's Garden) is one of Sofia's most desirable historic districts, with embassies, villas, and green streets. The area is quiet and prestigious, with good access to the center and transport.

**Public transport**: The apartment is a **6-minute walk (approximately 450 meters)** from **Metro Station "James Bourchier" (Line M2)** and on bus and tram routes along Tsarigradsko Shose and Evlogi Georgiev.

Nearby amenities within a 10-minute walk include:
- **South Park (Yuzhen Park)** and **Doctor's Garden** green area
- **Embassies** and diplomatic quarter
- **Supermarkets** (Billa, Fantastico) and local shops
- **Schools**, kindergartens, and medical practices
- **Cafés** and restaurants
