### Neighborhood & Location

Lozenets is widely regarded as one of Sofia's premium residential districts. The neighborhood is characterized by low-rise and mid-rise residential buildings, mature street trees, and an abundance of cafés, restaurants, boutique shops, and private medical clinics. It is popular with young professionals, families, and expatriates due to its central location and relatively quiet character compared to Mladost or Nadezhda.

**Public transport** is a key strength of this listing. The apartment is a **5-minute walk (approximately 400 meters)** from **Metro Station "Joliot-Curie" (Line M2)**, providing direct metro access to Sofia's city center (NDK station in under 10 minutes), the Sofia Airport, and the Business Park Sofia area. Additionally, tram lines 6 and 20 stop on the adjacent boulevard, with connections to the Orlov Most intersection and the National Palace of Culture.

Nearby amenities within a 10-minute walk include:
- **Billa supermarket** and **Kaufland** (5 and 8 minutes on foot, respectively)
- **South Park (Yuzhen Park)** — one of Sofia's largest urban parks, ideal for jogging, cycling, and family outings
- **Multiple private kindergartens and primary schools**, including 119th Primary School and the French Language High School
- **City Fitness Lozenets** gym
- **Several pharmacies and a DKC medical center**
