This two-bedroom apartment is distinguished by its **large 10 sq. m. balcony**, ideal for outdoor dining and relaxation. The property is on the third floor of a five-story building from 2003, in Hadzhi Dimitar (often grouped with Krasno Selo), a residential area with good metro access and local amenities.
