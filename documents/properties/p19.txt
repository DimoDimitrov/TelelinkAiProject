# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-019
## Three-Bedroom Apartment with Garden Access in Boyana, Sofia

---

### Overview

This three-bedroom apartment is in **Boyana**, a green, residential district at the foot of Vitosha Mountain, known for the Boyana Church (UNESCO) and a quieter, village-like atmosphere. The property is on the ground floor of a three-story building from 1992 and has **direct access to a shared garden** (communal green area for the building), a rare feature in Sofia. Ideal for families who value outdoor space and nature.

The apartment is at **ul. Boyansko Ezero 4, Boyana, Sofia 1616**, a quiet street with bus connection to the center. The asking price is **€135,000 (one hundred thirty-five thousand euros)**, reflecting the location, size, and garden access.

---

### Property Specifications

The total built-up area of the apartment is **115 square meters (sq. m.)**, with a usable living area of approximately **106 sq. m.** The layout comprises:

- **Three bedrooms**: Master (20 sq. m.) with built-in wardrobe and garden view; second (16 sq. m.) and third (12 sq. m.) suitable for children or study.
- **Living room / dining area**: 28 sq. m., with French doors to the **shared garden** (communal use, well maintained).
- **Kitchen**: Fully equipped, 11 sq. m., with cabinetry and appliances (2020).
- **Bathroom**: Full bathroom with bathtub and shower over bath, 6 sq. m., renovated in 2020.
- **Separate WC**: 2.5 sq. m.
- **Entrance hallway**: 8 sq. m. with storage.

The apartment is on the **ground floor (1st) out of 3**. No elevator. **Intercom** and **secure entrance**. **Garden access** via living room.

### Neighborhood & Location

Boyana is a residential district with single-family homes and low-rise blocks, greenery, and the **Boyana Church** and **National Museum of History** nearby. Connected by bus to the center and metro (change at terminal).

**Public transport**: The apartment is a **3-minute walk** from **bus stop "Boyana"** (lines 64, 66 to metro and center). **Metro**: Approx. 15–20 minutes by bus to **Metro Station "Vitosha" (Line M2)** or **"James Bourchier" (Line M2)**.

Nearby amenities within a 10-minute walk or short drive include:
- **Boyana Church** (UNESCO) and **National Museum of History**
- **Supermarkets** and local shops
- **Schools** and kindergartens
- **Vitosha Mountain** trails and nature
- **Restaurants** and cafés in the area
