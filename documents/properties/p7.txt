# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-007
## Two-Bedroom Apartment with Terrace in Krastova Vada, Sofia

---

### Overview

This attractive two-bedroom apartment stands out for its **private terrace** of 12 sq. m., a rare feature in the area. Located in Krastova Vada, a residential district with good transport links and a growing number of amenities, the property occupies the top (sixth) floor of a six-story building from 2005 and benefits from quiet, south-facing orientation and no overhead neighbours.

The apartment is at **ul. Krastova Vada 42, Krastova Vada, Sofia 1407**, a calm street within walking distance of the metro. The asking price is **€108,000 (one hundred eight thousand euros)**, reflecting the terrace, condition, and solid location.

---

### Property Specifications

The total built-up area of the apartment is **78 square meters (sq. m.)**, with a usable living area of approximately **72 sq. m.** (terrace 12 sq. m. additional). The layout comprises:

- **Two bedrooms**: Master bedroom (16 sq. m.) with south-facing window and built-in wardrobe; second bedroom (13 sq. m.) facing east.
- **Living room / dining area**: Open-plan space of 22 sq. m., with direct access to the **12 sq. m. terrace** (south).
- **Kitchen**: Fitted kitchen, 9 sq. m., with modern cabinetry and integrated appliances (2020).
- **Bathroom**: Full bathroom with shower cabin, 5.5 sq. m., renovated in 2020.
- **Entrance hallway**: 5 sq. m. with storage.

The apartment is on the **6th floor out of 6** (top floor), with **elevator access**. The building has an **intercom system** and **secure entrance**.

### Neighborhood & Location

Krastova Vada is a residential district with a mix of older and newer buildings, local shops, and good access to the metro and ring road. It is popular with families and commuters.

**Public transport**: The apartment is a **5-minute walk (approximately 380 meters)** from **Metro Station "Krastova Vada" (Line M2)**, with direct links to the city center and Sofia Airport. Bus lines 76 and 94 serve the area.

Nearby amenities within a 10-minute walk include:
- **Supermarkets** (Billa, Kaufland)
- **Primary school** and kindergartens
- **Pharmacies** and medical practices
- **Parks** and green spaces
- **Local cafés** and services
