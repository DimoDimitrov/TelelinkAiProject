# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-004
## Bright Two-Bedroom Apartment in Oborishte, Sofia

---

### Overview

This well-proportioned two-bedroom apartment is located in Oborishte, a central district known for its green streets and proximity to the city's administrative and cultural institutions. The property occupies the third floor of a four-story brick building from 1935, carefully restored and modernized, and offers high ceilings and period character combined with contemporary comfort.

The apartment is at **ul. Oborishte 15, Oborishte, Sofia 1504**, a quiet, tree-lined street near Borisova Gradina park. The asking price is **€128,000 (one hundred twenty-eight thousand euros)**, reflecting the prime location, building character, and recent quality renovation.

---

### Property Specifications

The total built-up area of the apartment is **85 square meters (sq. m.)**, with a usable living area of approximately **78 sq. m.** The layout comprises:

- **Two bedrooms**: The master bedroom (17 sq. m.) faces the street with high windows; the second bedroom (14 sq. m.) faces the courtyard. Both have built-in wardrobes.
- **Living room / dining area**: A bright space of 22 sq. m. with access to a 5 sq. m. balcony overlooking the courtyard garden.
- **Kitchen**: Modern fitted kitchen, 9 sq. m., with quality cabinetry and integrated appliances (2021).
- **Bathroom**: Full bathroom with bathtub and separate shower, 6 sq. m., fully renovated in 2021.
- **Entrance hallway**: 6 sq. m. with coat and storage space.

The apartment is on the **3rd floor out of 4**. The building has **no elevator** (low-rise). **Intercom system** and **refurbished entrance** with period-style tile work.

### Neighborhood & Location

Oborishte is one of Sofia's most desirable central districts, with a mix of pre-war and interwar architecture, embassies, and institutions. The area is green, quiet, and well served by transport and amenities.

**Public transport** is very good. The apartment is a **4-minute walk (approximately 350 meters)** from **Metro Station "Vasil Levski Stadium" (Line M2)** and within walking distance of tram and bus lines on Tsarigradsko Shose and Evlogi Georgiev Boulevard.

Nearby amenities within a 10-minute walk include:
- **Borisova Gradina** — Sofia's largest and most popular park
- **Vasil Levski National Stadium** and sports facilities
- **Supermarkets** (Billa, Fantastico) and local shops
- **Schools and kindergartens**, including 18th Primary School
- **Medical practices and pharmacies**
- **Embassies and cultural institutions**
