### Overview

This family-oriented three-bedroom apartment stands out for having **two full bathrooms**, a practical advantage for larger families or shared living. The property is on the sixth floor of an eight-story building from 1995, which was fully insulated in 2020. The layout offers a bright living area, three separate bedrooms, and a generous balcony.

The apartment is located at **bl. 302, ul. Akademik Georgi Bonchev 15, Druzhba, Sofia 1592**, within the residential complex and a short walk from the metro. The asking price is **€124,000 (one hundred twenty-four thousand euros)**, reflecting the size, two-bathroom layout, and condition.
