# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-008
## Spacious One-Bedroom with Home Office Niche in Sredets, Sofia

---

### Overview

This well-laid-out one-bedroom apartment in Sredets offers a **dedicated home office niche** off the living room, making it ideal for remote workers or professionals who need a quiet workspace. The property is on the first floor of a restored four-story building from the 1920s, with high ceilings and period details, situated in one of Sofia's most central and prestigious districts.

The apartment is located at **ul. Graf Ignatiev 22, Sredets, Sofia 1000**, steps from the main boulevards and institutions. The asking price is **€92,500 (ninety-two thousand five hundred euros)**, reflecting the central location, character, and recent quality renovation.
