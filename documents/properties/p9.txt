# Property Listing — REF: BG-SOF-009
## Three-Bedroom Family Apartment in Lyulin, Sofia

### Overview

This large three-bedroom apartment in Lyulin is well suited to families seeking space, parking, and good value within a quiet residential block. The property is on the fourth floor of a nine-story panel building from 1988, which was fully insulated and refurbished in 2021. The layout provides a clear separation between the living area and three bedrooms, with a generous balcony.

The apartment is located at **bl. 10, ul. Lyulin Planina 5, Lyulin, Sofia 1331**, within the residential complex and a short walk from the district center. The asking price is **€98,000 (ninety-eight thousand euros)**, offering strong value for a renovated three-bedroom with parking in this area.
